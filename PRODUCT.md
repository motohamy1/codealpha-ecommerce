# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Discerning shoppers seeking a high-end, curated shopping experience for premium/luxury goods. They expect a premium, high-fashion presentation that matches the quality and exclusiveness of the items they purchase.

## Product Purpose
LUXE is a premium e-commerce platform. It exists to offer an exceptional shopping experience where browsing and purchasing premium products feels like navigating a high-fashion, elite catalog. Success is evoking exclusivity and artistic quality while providing a seamless, elegant purchase flow.

## Brand Personality
Avant-Garde, Bold, and Brutalist. High-fashion, high-contrast, and striking artistic statements. Editorial layouts, stark contrasts, and dramatic type scale.

## Anti-references
- Typical AI design tells: cream-colored warm-neutral backgrounds, side-stripe borders, tiny tracked eyebrows above every section, ghost cards (1px border combined with large, soft shadows).
- Cluttered, discount-oriented storefronts: heavy promotional badges, sale countdown timers, flashy banners, or aggressive popups.
- Saturated SaaS clichés, over-rounded components (border-radius > 16px on cards), and soft, unconfident transitions.

## Design Principles
1. **Art as Interface**: The layout itself should feel like a piece of high-fashion print media, utilizing stark grids and dramatic asymmetries.
2. **Unapologetic Contrast**: Lean into bold blacks, pure whites, and deliberate high-contrast accents, rather than muted gray transitions.
3. **High-Fashion Editorial Rhythm**: Elevate typography to be the main visual driver, using dramatic scaling and intentional whitespace.
4. **Intentionality Over Decoration**: Avoid arbitrary accents (side-stripes, decorative badges, rounded cards). If an element is present, it must serve a structural or narrative purpose.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Strict compliance with WCAG 2.1 AA.
- Color contrast ratio ≥ 4.5:1 for body text and normal text, ≥ 3:1 for large text.
- Complete keyboard accessibility for interactive elements.
- Full respect for user prefers-reduced-motion queries with alternate instant or crossfade transitions.
